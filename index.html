Background

I was 13 and in junior high school the year that Tim Berners-Lee invented the World Wide Web.
I recall learning to type and playing Oregon Trail on the Apple Computers at my school, and all library research was done with the Dewey Decimal System.
In 1991, Tim Berners-Lee invented HTML. I was a Sophomore in high school. Needless to say, web design was not yet an elective.
My senior year, there was a computer in the house. I didn't know how to use it. My Dad used it for work, I think we were online, but I didn't know how to use it.
Somehow, my highschool education ended right as the WWW was beginning, and my interests were not computer science related, so it took me a long time to find myself here, learning web development.
